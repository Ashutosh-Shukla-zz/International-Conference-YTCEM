About the Conference

Yadavrao Tasgaonkar College of Engineering & Management, Bhivpuri Road, Karjat, Mumbai University, State: Maharashtra, India is organizing International Conference on "EMERGING TRENDS IN TECHNOLOGY AND IT'S APPLICATIONS", on 7th - 8th March 2014.


Scope of the Conference

The Conference Provides a leading forum for sharing the original research contributions and practical development experiences among researchers and application developers.


Theme of the Conference

The international Conference on “Emerging Trends in Technology and its Applications” (ICETTA-2014) will focuses on recent the trends in the field of Technology. Papers are invited on the following major Tracks of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Biomedical Engineering & Applied Sciences.


Objective of the Conference ICETTA-2014

The objective of the conference is to provide global platform to the academicians Professionals and practitioners who are actually involved in research activity in the field of Engineering and Technology to contribute and to disseminate the innovations and research new work. The Conference Provides a leading forum for the original research contributions and practical development experiences among researchers and application developers.
