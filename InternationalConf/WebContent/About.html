About Us:


Dr. Nandkumar Tasgaonkar –

A young, dynamic medical professional in Radiology formed the Saraswati Education Society in the year 2003 to progress and execute his philanthropic with the clear objective to open doors of progress to the rural youth. His vision to look beyond the horizon paved the way for the development of education in the Karjat Taluka. With in the short span of eight years SES started eleven institutes which consist of three engineering colleges, two polytechnics, two Pharmacy institutes, CBSE school and four Management institute with the excellent back up of laboratories, rich collection of book, Wi-Fi Campus, etc.

             The globalization of the world economy has thrown challenges to all and education is not exception to that. We at YTGOI accepted the challenges of the globalized economy to develop and take education at the doorstep of the poorest amongst the poor rural youth and bring them into the mainstream of the national life.
