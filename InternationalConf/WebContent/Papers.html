Call for Papers


We invites abstract from the distinguish authors, researcher, students, Faculty members, academicians their unpublished research work & experimental results relating to following unlimited thematic areas:



1.   ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

VLSI & Embedded System, Telecommunications, Wireless Communication and Networks, Network Protocols, Renewable Energy Technology, Microelectronics and Semiconductor Engineering, ICMOS Technology, Bio chips, Nano Technology, Robotics and Automation, RF Microwave Technique, Antennas and Propagation, Optical Communications, Signal Processing for Communication, Channel Coding, Multimedia Communications, Remote Sensing and Applications, Data Compression and Encryption.



2.   MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Manufacturing and Production Processes, Renewable Energy and MEMS and Nanotechnology, Energy Engineering and Management, Material science & Processing, CAD/CAM/CIM/CFD, Computational Techniques, Automotive, Industrial, Aerodynamics, Automation Engineering, Machatronics & Robotics Engineering Thermodynamics, Product Design and Development. Fluid Mechanics, Internal Combustion Engines, Surface Engineering, Metal Joining Processes, Composite Materials, Robotics and Mechatronics, Project Management ,Smart Structure, Fluid Structure Analysis, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Finite Element Analysis, Supply change management, operation research.



3.   CIVIL ENGINEERING

Water resources, Disaster Management, Geological and Geotechnical Engineering, Structural Engineering and Disaster Management, Water Purification Technologies, Urban Planning, Energy Economics, Environmental Impact Assessment, Concrete Technology, Microbial Geotechnics.



4.   ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

Energy conservation Management, Industrial Drives and Special Machines, Power Drives and Control, Brushless DC Motor Drives, Energy Harvesting and Storage, Virtual instrumentation and control Analysis, Design control and applications of Electrical machines and drives, Power Quality in grids with distributed generation and Renewable Energies, Smart Grid Road map in India, Soft-Switching static power converters, Optimization Techniques in Electrical Systems, Restructuring and Electricity Markets, FACTS devices Modeling and Application Design, Analysis, Modeling and Control of Power Electronic Systems. Controls System Modeling and Simulation



5.   COMPUTER SCIENCE & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Artificial Intelligence, Neural Network and Fuzzy System, Image Processing, Real-Time Computing, Cyber Security, Network and System Security, Web Services, Cloud Computing and Grid Computing, Parallel Computing and Distributed Computing, Pattern recognition, E-governance, Data Mining, Wireless Sensor Networks, Mobile Computing, Service Oriented Architecture, Advances in Data Base Management System, Software Engineering , Business Intelligence.



6.   BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING

Rehabilitation & Assistive Technologies. Bioinstrumentation. Robotics in Medicine. Biomaterials and Tissue Engineering, Nano-Biotechnology. BIOMEMS, Image processing in Biomedical Engineering, Advances in Regenerative Medicine and Stem Cell Engineering, Bio process modeling and simulation, Bio safety and Bioethics.



7.   ENGINEERING SCIENCE & MATHEMATICS

Chemistry, Physics, Applied Mathematics, Quantitative Techniques, Numerical Methods, Random Process, Transform theory, Statistics, Environmental Science.
